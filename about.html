Welcome to Amruthvahini College of Engineering

Amrutvahini College of Engineering (AVCOE), Sangamner established in 1983 under the leadership of Hon. Late. Bhausaheb Thorat and progessing under the guidance of Hon. Shri. Balasaheb Thorat, Ex-Revenue Minister,Government of Maharashtra and Hon. Dr.Sudhir Tambe (Ex-MLC Nashik). It is spread over 15.6 acres of ample greenery, with four times accreditation by NBA, New Delhi, Graded 'A+' by NACC Government of India and Educational Organization Management System(EOMS)in accordance with ISO 21001:2018. It boasts state of art IT centre with 500 MBPs Leased Line and 300 MBPs Broadband Internet Facility. It has a huge library that accommodates 69,309 books,44000+ e-Books & 315 e-journals. It provides 100% placement assistance to its students through value added courses,foreign langauges (Japanese , German) and Training. It is an Institute that has always worked for imparting excellence driven engineering education that builds the future of Engineers and Entrepreneurs.
